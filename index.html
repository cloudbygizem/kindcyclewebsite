Give unused items a second life in your local Irish community.

KindCycle is a simple, entry-level charity project that connects people in Ireland who have items they no longer use with neighbours who really need them – from Dublin to Galway, Cork to Limerick.

Small acts of kindness, circling through our towns and villages.

Donate an item

How KindCycle works

  • You offer a clean, usable item that you no longer need.
  • Our small volunteer team matches it with a local person or family in Ireland.
  • Together you agree a safe pick-up or drop-off location, such as a community centre or café.

Vision & Mission

Our vision

A kinder Ireland where good quality items stay in the cycle of use, reducing waste and supporting people who are facing difficult times.

Our mission

To make it easy for anyone in Ireland to donate everyday items in a safe, transparent and respectful way – starting with a simple online form and community-based handovers.
