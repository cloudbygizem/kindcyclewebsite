How KindCycle Works

  • You **offer an item** in good condition.
  • We **match it** with someone in need in Ireland.
  • You agree a **collection point** (libraries, community cafés, GAA clubs).

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

A kinder Ireland where unused items are recirculated with respect and care.

Mission

To make giving effortless so items flow through Irish towns and support those who need them most.
